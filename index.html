Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Muzaffar Technologies (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the QR Code Scanner application (“App”).

Information We Collect

  • Camera Access: Used only to scan QR codes. No images or video are stored or transmitted.
  • Scan Content: Processed locally. No data is sent to external servers unless explicitly shared by the user.
  • Optional Diagnostics: Anonymous crash reports and usage statistics may be collected to improve app performance.

Permissions

  • Camera: Required for scanning QR codes.
  • Network: Used only when opening scanned URLs or submitting optional diagnostics.
  • Storage: Used to save scan history locally if enabled by the user.

Data Usage

We use collected data solely to provide and improve the app’s functionality. We do not sell or share personal data.

Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • Disable camera access via device settings.
  • Clear scan history from within the app.
  • Opt out of diagnostics in the app settings.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted within the app and on our website.
